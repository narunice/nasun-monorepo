========== THE MARKET SECTION ==========
[Title]
The Market

[Intro]
The unified DEX thesis is proven. The differentiation battle is just beginning.

[Market Point 1]
[Title] $100B+ in DeFi TVL
[Description] Spread across 500+ protocols on dozens of chains. Users manage
multiple wallets, bridge between networks, and accept fragmented risk exposure
just to access basic financial services.

[Market Point 2]
[Title] Unified Finance Is Table Stakes
[Description] Hyperliquid, dYdX v4, and Jupiter have already consolidated
trading verticals. Unified margin is no longer a differentiator — it is a
minimum requirement. The market has moved past whether to unify, to how to
retain users after unifying.

[Market Point 3]
[Title] The Real Gap
[Description] Every unified DEX solves capital fragmentation. None solves
community fragmentation. Users trade on Hyperliquid and discuss on Discord.
The social context lives outside the product. Pado is the first platform
where community forms inside the trading interface.

[Market Point 4]
[Title] Own L1 = Full Stack Control
[Description] By running on Nasun — a dedicated Sui-forked L1 — Pado controls
consensus, execution, fee structure, and upgrade cadence. Zero dependency on
external chain governance or congestion.


========== THE PRODUCT SECTION ==========
[Title]
The Product

[Intro]
Pado is a self-custodial unified finance application live on Nasun Devnet.

[Product Highlight 1]
[Title] Finance-First Social
[Description] The primary differentiator. Community gathers inside the product,
not in a Discord server. Chat embedded in trading pairs, leaderboards that drive
competition, prediction markets that turn conviction into conversation. No feed.
No followers. No likes. Just markets and the people in them.

[Product Highlight 2]
[Title] 7 Products, 1 Account
[Description] Spot trading, perpetual futures, lending, prediction markets,
lottery, staking, and payments — all accessible from a single smart account
with shared collateral.

[Product Highlight 3]
[Title] CEX-Grade UX, DEX-Level Transparency
[Description] zkLogin enables 30-second onboarding with no seed phrase. All
execution is fully on-chain and verifiable. Users get exchange-grade convenience
without surrendering custody.

[Product Highlight 4]
[Title] Unified Capital Infrastructure
[Description] Portfolio-level risk management across all products. Multi-collateral
support with tiered risk weights. This is the infrastructure layer that keeps
capital flowing — and keeps users from needing to leave.

========== BUSINESS MODEL SECTION ==========
[Title]
Business Model

[Intro]
Multiple revenue streams that scale with platform adoption.

[Revenue Stream 1]
[Title] Trading Fees
[Description] Maker/taker fees on spot and perpetual markets. Primary revenue
driver that scales directly with trading volume.

[Revenue Stream 2]
[Title] Lending Spread
[Description] Protocol takes a spread between lending and borrowing rates.
Generates passive revenue proportional to lending pool utilization.

[Revenue Stream 3]
[Title] Lottery Treasury
[Description] 10% of lottery ticket sales allocated to protocol treasury.
Provides consistent revenue from a high-engagement product.

[Revenue Stream 4]
[Title] Premium Features (Future)
[Description] Copy trading subscriptions, AI agent access, and strategy
marketplace commissions. Second-order revenue enabled by platform network effects.

[Revenue Stream 5]
[Title] NFT Sales (Community Round)
[Description] Genesis NFT collection as the first community funding mechanism.
Provides early liquidity while aligning incentives between team and community.
